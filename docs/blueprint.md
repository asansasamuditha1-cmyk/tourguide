# **App Name**: LankaGuide AI

## Core Features:

- AI Tour Planning Tool: AI-powered trip planner which generates personalized Sri Lanka tour itineraries based on user preferences such as interests, budget, and duration. The tool incorporates real-time data such as weather, traffic, and event schedules.
- Location-Aware Recommendations: Provide instant recommendations for nearby attractions, restaurants, and activities using the phone's location services.  Prioritize the recommendations using AI, based on popularity, user reviews, and relevance to user's interests.
- Augmented Reality (AR) Overlays: Integrate AR overlays on the phone's camera view to provide historical information, reviews, and interactive experiences related to landmarks and points of interest.
- Offline Maps and Content: Enable users to download maps, itineraries, and relevant information for offline access, reducing dependency on internet connectivity.
- Social Sharing: Allow users to share their tour experiences, photos, and reviews with friends and family through social media integration.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FF7F50) evoking the warmth and energy of Sri Lanka.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), a neutral backdrop that ensures readability and highlights the vibrancy of the primary color.
- Accent color: Deep turquoise (#40E0D0) for interactive elements and calls to action, inspired by the ocean surrounding the island.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), combining modernity and a touch of warmth for comfortable reading.
- Use custom icons that reflect Sri Lankan culture and landmarks; keep icons simple, clear, and easily recognizable.
- Prioritize a clean and intuitive layout, emphasizing visual content such as photos and maps; utilize a card-based design for easy browsing.
- Employ subtle animations to enhance user engagement and provide feedback on interactions, such as smooth transitions and loading indicators.
